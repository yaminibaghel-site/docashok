Understanding colostomy

Type 03 · Urinary

Understanding urostomy

Diverts urine from a diseased bladder to an external pouch — ileal and cecal conduits, and daily life.

Our mission

Envisioning near-normal lives.

An ostomy is crucial for a patient's health, but it brings real changes to body image and lifestyle. For many — especially in regions with less education and support — adjusting to this "new normal" raises worries about the future and daily life.

Ostomy Care India works to close that gap: providing information and education, collaborating with healthcare professionals on the latest techniques, and advocating so every ostomate feels informed, supported and empowered.
