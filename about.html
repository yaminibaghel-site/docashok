About Dr. Ashok

Dr. Ashok Kumar

MBBS, MS, MCh, FRCS, FACS, FICS, FASCRS, FACRSI, FAMSI — Former Professor, Department of Surgical Gastroenterology, SGPGI, Lucknow.

Profile

A surgeon-scientist, educator and mentor.

Dr. Ashok obtained his MBBS and MS degrees from Banaras Hindu University, and went on to specialise (MCh) in Surgical Gastroenterology at the Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences (SGPGI) in 1995.

He has supervised 5 PhD students, served as co-supervisor to 4 PhD candidates and several MD students and post-doctoral fellows, and continues to pursue research alongside patient care and the teaching of GI-surgery postgraduates.

Current affiliations: Professor, Department of Surgery, T.S. Misra Medical College, Lucknow · Consultant Surgical Gastroenterologist, Charak Hospital & Research Center, Lucknow.
