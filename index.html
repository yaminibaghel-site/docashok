About the surgeon

A 25-year record in surgical gastroenterology.

Dr. Ashok Kumar has devoted over two decades to advancing surgical gastroenterology in India — combining complex operative care with prolific research and the mentorship of PhD scholars, post-doctoral fellows and GI-surgery postgraduates.

Currently Professor in the Department of Surgery at T.S. Misra Medical College and Consultant Surgical Gastroenterologist at Charak Hospital & Research Center, Lucknow — and formerly Professor of Surgical Gastroenterology at SGPGI, Lucknow.

Clinical focus

Two fields, treated with depth.

A practice built around the colon and rectum, peri-anal disease, and the biliary–pancreatic system.

  • Surgery for colon and rectal cancer

  • Surgery for ulcerative colitis & Crohn's disease
  • Surgery for rectal prolapse and constipation
  • Surgery for faecal incontinence
  • Repair of traumatic anal sphincter injury
  • Surgery for fissure-in-ano
  • Stapled haemorrhoidopexy

  • Surgery for choledochal cyst

  • Management of common bile duct stones
